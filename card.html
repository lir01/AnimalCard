Rabbits

Rabbits are vegan, their diet is 100% plant based

  • Scientific classification: Mammal
  • Number of breeds: 305
  • Habitat: Woods, forests, grasslands

Rabbits have sharp senses of hearing and vision; their sharp senses allow them to detect threat early and acct quickly. Rabbits are social animals who enjoy being in a herd. They express joy by jumping and twisting in the air.
